What We Do

  • Based on the information you provide; we will match you with your partner.
  • We will then email you about the information about your partner.
  • The volunteer is expected to reach out to their partner first through email and later can switch to a more comfortable platform such as Discord, WhatsApp, Viber, Telegram etc.
  • During the session, please ensure that both parties switch on their camera for efficient communication.

    Further information regarding the first session and session materials please refer this page.

How It's Done

  • The volunteer is expected to hold one 60-minute session OR two 30-minute sessions per week at minimum with their respective partner.
  • If the learner wants have more sessions per week, make a request to the volunteer. If the volunteer is unavailable or is not willing to do more than one hour per week then please contact us. We will gladly arrange a solution for you.
  • You can converse on day-to-day topics of your choice OR use the session materials provided by us.
  • Keep the sessions interactive, casual, lively and fun.
  • Treat your partner as your friend.
