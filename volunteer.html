Volunteers

Why Join ENG@sl ?

  1. We provide you with an outlet to blend with different socio-cultural groups and ethnicities, an ideal opportunity to familiarize on their thoughts, attitude towards life, their pattern of thinking and their sense of individuality.

  2. People are matched according to their similar traits and will be of similar age groups ensuring safe and long-lasting friendships with common ground for interaction and thus will have a variety of topics to converse on.

  3. An incredible chance to develop your confidence, communication skills and presentation skills effortlessly.

  4. We verify your volunteer hours and provide you with a certificate or/and a letter of acknowledgement with the number of hours you have committed to help your buddy at ENG@sl.

  5. Achieve self-satisfaction when you witness your buddy’s progression.

  6. Flexible volunteering hours – minimum 1 hour commitment per week.

Are You Eligible ?

All of our volunteers must meet the following requirements in order to apply for our program.

  1. Volunteers must be of age 14 or older.

  2. Volunteers must be native English speakers or fluent English speaker.
    (B2 – C2)

How To Join Us

  1. Fill In The Application Form
    Fill out our Volunteer Application. We use the information you provide in order to match you with a student.
  2. Interview
    Once we have read your application we will reach out to you within a week or so in order to schedule a meeting with you.
