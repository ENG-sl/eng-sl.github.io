Students

Why Join ENG@sl ?

It’s only natural that people tend to forget what they have learnt with time, especially if your immediate surrounding consists of people who do not use English on a daily basis. Therefore, ENG@sl will provide you with the best surrounding to improve your language skills on a regular basis.

Benefits For Students

  1. Free of charge service.

  2. Learners are provided with a friendly and interactive platform where they will receive individual attention during their one-to-one sessions. The volunteer will help to improve your grammar, pronunciation and vocabulary.

  3. People are matched according to their similar traits and will be of similar age groups ensuring safe and long-lasting friendships with common ground for interaction and thus will have a variety of topics to converse on.

  4. If requested we provide a letter of acknowledgement on the number of hours you have committed to practice English with ENG@sl.

  5. Your progress would be regularly evaluated by our team ensuring that you receive the maximum benefit possible.

  6. We provide you maximum flexibility on the number of hours you wish to commit on improving yourself at ENG@sl. If you wish to have more sessions per week, make a request to the volunteer. If the volunteer is unavailable or is not willing to do more than one hour per week then please contact us. We will gladly arrange a solution for you.

Eligibility

  1. Students must be of age 14 or older.

  2. Students should have a basic knowledge on the language of English. In fact if you understand the conversation in the following video upto a great extent, you are eligilbe to enroll as a student.

How To Join Us

  1. Fill In The Application Form
    Fill out our Student Application. Once we go through your application, we will reach out to you.
  2. Interview
    One of our representatives will interview you. Information gathered throughout this process will only be used for the matching process.
