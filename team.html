Who We Are

We are a group of passionate and creative young minds with a goal of helping people all around the world improve thier fluency in the English Language.
This project started when we realised that being fluent in English is largely beneficial in the present as well as in the future. As English has become one of the most popular languages used around the world, so has opportunities that come with being fluent in English has increased.
